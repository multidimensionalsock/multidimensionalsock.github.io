Who am I?

My names Robyn, I am currently a third year Games Design and Programming student with experience using C++ and C#, both inside of engines such as Unity and Unreal as well as outside of engines using relevant software libraries to make software using Visual Studio. I aspire to go into a job working as a game play or AI programmer in the industry. 

Throughout my time at University I have developed a multitude of projects, some of which you can see above as well as personal endeavors. For both of the collaborative projects I have worked on I have acted as Project Lead, first to a team of 8 and later to a team of 21, with people coming from a range of specializations. In my final year collaborative project I am working both as project lead as well as lead designer for the game, developing and implementing the story line and mechanics as well as breaking down, scoping and delegating task throughout the design and tech team, while facilitating communication with the art teams. 

I enjoy bug fixing and helping others with solving complex problems. One of the main draws of games for me is the abundance of different problems there is to solve, where none will be the same even between games. Games combine art and technology in a way that very few is any other mediums can, creating interactive stories and worlds that we get to form and affect as we wish. I see programming as a form of art in itself, and I pride myself on efficient and readable code by looking at lots of different ways to solve a problem to find the best one.

Robyn Botham 2024
