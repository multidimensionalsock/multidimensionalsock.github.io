Who am I?

I'm a recently Graduated Games design and programming student. I have experience using Unity (C#) and Unreal 5 (blueprint and C++) as well as using multiple libraries and frameworks to develop software in visual studio using C++ and C#. I am a quick learning and if a project requires me to learn a new skillset, engine, or programming language I am fully willing to put the hours into learning it, and have done so before.

I'm most interested in creating adventure and RPG style games, but I get most of my enjoyment out of problem solving, so am willing to work on anything and everything. I enjoy debugging and getting to the heart of an issue and solving it from the ground up.

I have worked before in small teams, often acting as the team lead which has given me expereince in project management, organization and time keeping both for myself and others. Working in teams helps me produce my best work and I can't wait to get into the industry to learn from others.
